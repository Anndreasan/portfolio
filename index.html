About Me

I'm Andreas Andersson, a UX/UI Designer

I am interested in color and form, and have chosen to pursue a career in UX design with a focus on requirements expertise. With a background in retail and furniture craftsmanship, it's now time for me to bring the world more visually appealing and user friendly digital experiences.

For several years, I have worked in retail as a logistics specialist, merchandiser, salesperson, store manager, and project manager. During this time, I have developed strong communication skills and the ability to quickly adapt to changing situations. I am accustomed to working with people and solving problems, which I consider to be central components of the role of a UX designer. My experience has given me a deep understanding of meeting user needs and creating a positive user experience.

By combining my artistic sensibilities with technical know-how, I aim to craft seamless and intuitive experiences that delight users and fulfill their needs. In this continually developing digital landscape, I am excited to contribute my unique perspective and skills to create meaningful connections between people and technology.

What i do

Creative ideas

I craft user experiences that are both enjoyable and intuitive. By choosing effective color schemes, adding playful details, and employing smart navigation, I create interfaces that are visually appealing and user-friendly, balancing aesthetics with functionality for seamless interactions.

Modern materials

In today's IT landscape, I leverage cutting-edge materials like digital interface elements and 3D animations to craft dynamic, interactive user experiences. These innovations allow me to create designs that not only engage users but also evolve with the latest technological advancements.

Tools

I rely on Figma to create user-centered, visually compelling products and services. With its powerful features and intuitive interface, Figma empowers me to experiment, visualize, and test ideas and concepts effectively within UX design.

Unique approach

I bring innovative ideas to UX design, making apps and websites enjoyable to use. By incorporating helpful elements and embracing the latest technologies, I craft distinctive and engaging interfaces that truly stand out.

SEO

I optimize websites for search engines to improve visibility and drive organic traffic. By leveraging effective keywords and best practices, I ensure that content reaches the right audience and ranks higher in search results.

Art

I love using color and shapes to create images and posters. Being creative and exploring artistic expression is a key part of my work, allowing me to bring unique and engaging visuals to life.
